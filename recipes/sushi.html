To make the best sushi you need to have the best raw fish

Ingredients:

  • Raw Salmon
  • Raw Tuna
  • Rice
  • Avocado
  • Nori Wrap

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Let the rice cool for 10 mins
  3. Place rice on nori wrap and spread evenly
  4. add avocado and salmon
  5. roll and cut in slices
